Dry Docking Services

Dry Docking Management

Our technical team fully manages the planning, preparation, and execution of dry-docking for vessels of any tonnage, whether scheduled or unscheduled. Dry docking is one of the most significant expenses for ship owners throughout a vessel's lifetime. Without proper operation, controls, and expert management, costs can escalate rapidly, and dock time can overrun, impacting upcoming operational expenses.

With an experienced team, we ensure the entire repair job is completed within the approved budget and timeframe, minimizing vessel downtime and enabling a swift return to regular operations. Our proven track record in successful dry docking and last-minute repairs demonstrates the effectiveness of our "hands-on" approach, optimizing overall expenses.
