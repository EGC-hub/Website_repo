Our History

Brief History

ABC Shipping Company was established in 1980 as a ship chandler in the United Arab Emirates. Over the past four decades, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in the marine industry, serving clients across the Middle East with dedication and excellence.

From our humble beginnings, we have expanded our offerings to include a wide range of marine services, technical support, and safety inspections, all while maintaining our core values of quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
