Discover Wadi Shab: Hidden Gems & Stunning Sinkhole

A journey that will take visitors to Wadi Shap and Sink Hole. Travelling for two hours by car one way from 8 in the morning will reach Wadi Shap.

Climb aboard the boat to reach the distant bank of the river. The beginning of the adventure starts with a forty-five-minute walking journey until you reach a blue swimming pool where you can make the aquatic journey to observe the waterfall inside a cave. Users must bring waterproof shoes and swimwear and should choose suitable hiking footwear for the journey.

Driving to the sinkhole near Wadi Shap on the road to Muscat requires 15 minutes travel time.
