Desert Safari Adventure with Camel Rides

An activity awaits for adventurers at the desert safari which delivers the genuine Arabian wild experience. The desert safari experience starts with an adrenaline-packed drive across the soothing hills of sand using 4x4 vehicles then leads to riding a camel while guided by professional Bedouin nomads. The cultural encounter reaches its authentic peak thanks to camel sightings with their traditional saddles which adorn their bodies with vibrant colors. While visiting the desert you should appreciate panoramic views between the extensive sand seas coupled with the dramatic sky's deep blue color. The daytime excursion brings an authentic look into the landscape's natural appeals as well as traditional culture and results in lifelong memories.

Itinerary

Experience a dramatic adventure with 4x4 dune bash and Bedouin-led camel ride through the desert which provides exclusive sights of the scene. Spend time at a desert camp afterward while exhaling the authentic atmosphere of the place and taking in refreshments. Experience a powerful sunset from the dunes that perfectly represents desert peacefulness. Your Arabian desert experience finishes with a drive to return to your origin while carrying memories of this perfect day spent in nature's beautiful desert landscape.
